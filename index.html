Marathon Race Number and Time Assignment

Welcome to the marathon race number and time assignment page. Below you will find the details of your race number and the time you are scheduled to race based on your registration status and age.

Instructions

To find out your race number and time, please open the console in your browser's developer tools. You can change the variables registeredEarly and runnerAge in the marathon.js file to see how your race number and time will be assigned based on different scenarios.

Race Number Assignment

Your race number is generated based on whether you registered early and your age. The race number is assigned as follows:

  • If you are an adult (18 or older) and registered early, your race number will be your base number plus 1000.
  • If you are an adult (18 or older) and registered late, your race number will be your base number.
  • If you are a youth (under 18) and registered early, your race number will be your base number plus 2000.
  • If you are a youth (under 18) and registered late, your race number will be your base number plus 3000.

Race Time Assignment

Your race time is assigned based on your registration status and age. The race times are as follows:

  • If you are an adult (18 or older) and registered early, you will race at 9:30 A.M.
  • If you are an adult (18 or older) and registered late, you will race at 11:00 A.M.
  • If you are a youth (under 18) and registered early, you will race at 12:30 P.M.
  • If you are a youth (under 18) and registered late, you will race at 5:30 P.M.

Thank you for participating in the marathon! We wish you the best of luck in your race.
